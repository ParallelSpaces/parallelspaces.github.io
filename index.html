Finally on Mac

The feature your Mac was always missing.

Other platforms have let you run two copies of the same app for years. Mac never did — until now. Parallel Spaces brings true multi-instance to macOS the way it should have been all along: native, instant, and built to feel like the rest of the system.

  • Native — not a wrapper. Your real apps, as many copies as you want.
  • No virtual machine, no container. Zero overhead.
  • Made for macOS. Sandboxed, distributed through the App Store.
  • For the multi-account life. Without the hacks and workarounds.

How it works

A new space in four steps.

No setup files, no terminal, no virtual machine. Just pick an app and go.

1

Pick an app

Choose any app on your Mac — or turn a website into one.

2

Name your space

Give it a name, a colour and a badge so you always know which is which.

3

Parallel Spaces builds it

It creates a separate launcher with its own private, isolated storage.

4

Launch and sign in

It opens as its own app, with its own account. Make as many as you need.

Works with your apps

Two ways to make a space.

Parallel Spaces supports the apps already on your Mac — and any website you want to treat like one.

Web apps

Any website, as its own app

Gmail, WhatsApp Web, Notion, ChatGPT, your CRM — give each one a real Mac app: a Dock icon, its own window, and its own isolated storage.

  • Native macOS notifications — never miss a message
  • Run the same site multiple times, each with a different login
  • Fully separate cookies and sessions per space
  • Feels like a first-class app, not a browser tab
Native apps

The apps you already installed

Messaging clients, browsers, Electron apps and many more. Parallel Spaces detects how each app stores its data and isolates a second copy correctly.

  • Works with Chromium browsers, Electron apps and beyond
  • Each space keeps its own profile and preferences
  • Smart auto-detection of the right isolation method
  • Power-user controls when you want them
